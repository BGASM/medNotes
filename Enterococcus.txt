Enterococcus: Gram+ cocci in pairs and short chains, alpha- or non-hemolytic, catalase-negative, PYR-positive.
    Cause:  - Primary cause of nosocomial infections
            - Urinary tract infections
            - Peritonitis
            - Bacteremia
            - Endocarditis

    Concern:    - E. faecalis and E. faecium

    Treatment:
        - Very difficult to treat because they have gained resistances rapidly.
        - Ampicillin/Vancomycin + Gentamycin for endocarditis or severe infections
        - Linezolid
        - Daptomycin
        - Tigecycline
